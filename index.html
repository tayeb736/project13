Who We Are

A Team of Passionate Builders

Cycode is an enterprise-grade software development company based in Constantine, Algeria. Founded in 2026, we bring together specialists in frontend, backend, design, mobile, AI, and cybersecurity to deliver complete digital solutions for clients worldwide.

We believe the best products come from collaborative minds. Every project is a shared effort — from initial wireframes to final deployment and security auditing. Our team of 8 specialists has delivered 50+ projects with a 99% uptime guarantee.

Enterprise-Grade Security

Security isn't an afterthought — it's baked into everything we build. From code review to deployment, every step follows rigorous security protocols.

  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest
  • Regular penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-factor authentication and RBAC implementation
  • Automated security scanning in CI/CD pipeline
  • Compliance with international data protection standards
  • 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
